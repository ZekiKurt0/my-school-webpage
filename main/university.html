ABOUT US

Established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette No. 30425 dated May 18, 2018, Eskişehir Technical University has 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 College and 3 campuses in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, a city of science, culture and also youth. It consists of the Institute and 6 Research and Application Centers.
Eskisehir Technical University works to prepare its students as the most equipped and competitive individuals for the future in creative and dynamic educational environments, in its units with a strong infrastructure where it carries out education and research activities, and with its competent teaching staff, each of whom follows the developments in their field and shares all their time with their students. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in the 2 Eylül Campus of our University, is carried out by our Faculty of Aviation and Astronautics. There are international flights operated by different airline companies on various days of the week to Hasan Polatkan Airport.




INFORMATION ABOUT STUDENTS AND STAFF

There are a total of 745 academic staff, 893 administrative staff and 11579 students at Eskişehir Technical University.Information about the distribution of these numbers is as follows.
Among the academic staff, 181 are professors, 74 are associate professors, 200 are lecturers, 159 are lecturers and 131 are research assistants. Among the administrative staff, 297 are permanent civil servants, 117 contracted civil servants and 477 are workers. Among the students, 7685 are studying in faculties, 2251 are in vocational schools and 1643 are in institutes.
