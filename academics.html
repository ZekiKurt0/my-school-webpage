Faculties

There are a total of 5 faculties at Eskişehir Technical University and their names are as follows:
  • FACULTY OF ENGINEERING
    • This faculty consists of a total of 8 departments and the number of students studying is 3176.
  • FACULTY OF SCIENCE
    • This faculty consists of a total of 8 departments and the number of students studying is 1323.
  • FACULTY OF ARCHITECTURE AND DESIGN
    • This faculty consists of a total of 4 departments and the number of students studying is 1429.
  • FACULTY OF SPORT SCIENCES
    • This faculty consists of a total of 4 departments and the number of students studying is 706.
  • FACULTY OF AERONAUTICS AND SPACE SCIENCES
    • This faculty consists of a total of 5 departments and the number of students studying is 1035.

    Institutes
    Eskişehir Technical University has a total of 3 institutes, their names are as follows:
  • INSTITUTION OF GRADUATE SCHOOL
    • In this institute, students can progress towards master's degree, doctorate or proficiency in arts. The number of students is 1645.
  • INSTITUTE OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION SCIENCES
    • This institute is the Institute of Transportation Sciences is the first and only institute in its field.
  • EARTH AND SPACE SCIENCES INSTITUTE
    • Within the Eskişehir Technical University Institute of Earth and Space Sciences, studies are carried out in three departments, especially R&D activities, education and training, and social benefit departments.

    Vocational Higher Schools
    Eskişehir Technical University has a total of 2 vocational higher school, their names are as follows:
  • PORSUK VOCATIONAL SCHOOL
    • Porsuk Vocational School provides education in 9 branches and the number of students is 1115.
  • VOCATIONAL SCHOOL OF TRANSPORTATION
    • Vocational School of Transportation provides education in 3 departments in 8 programs. The number of students is 2237.
